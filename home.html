About Me

I'm a passionate UI/UX designer and web developer with a strong foundation in creating user-centered digital experiences. Currently pursuing my degree in Computer Science and Software Engineering, I combine technical skills with creative design thinking to solve complex problems.

My journey in design began with a deep curiosity about how users interact with digital products. I believe that great design is invisible it just works. Through my education at SLIIT and hands-on experience, I've developed expertise in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and front-end development.

When I'm not designing or coding, you can find me creating content on YouTube, sharing my knowledge and experiences with the design community. I'm always eager to take on new challenges and collaborate on innovative projects.
